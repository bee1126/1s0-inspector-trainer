1S0 Inspector Trainer

Interactive safety training for Air Force inspectors.

A mission-ready training companion that blends standards-aligned lessons with scenario-based learning, quick checks, and a Duolingo-style XP and streak loop.

  • Interactive modules (LOTO, Fall Protection, RM, Hazard Abatement, RAC, and more)
  • Scenario-driven decision training with instant feedback and time pressure mode
  • Quick checks with completion summaries
  • XP, levels, daily goals, streaks, and badges to build habits
  • On-device progress tracking (no accounts)
