Support

For help, questions, or feedback about 1S0 Inspector Trainer, contact: abdoulbah1126@gmail.com

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • Device model + iOS version
  • The module/scenario name
  • Steps to reproduce
  • What you expected vs. what happened

Tip: The app includes built-in “Bug Report” and “Feature Request” forms under the Feedback tab to help you format a report.

For a DAF e-Publishing problem, use Refs → Live DAF e-Pubs → Report Broken Publication Link. Some government or managed networks may require the publication to be opened in the system browser. A reachability indicator confirms only that the server responded; always verify the PDF title page, incorporated changes, guidance memoranda, supplements, and local procedures before field use.
