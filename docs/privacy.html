Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 1S0 Inspector Trainer (“the app”) handles information when you use it.

Information We Collect

The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information by default. Training progress (such as completed modules and scores), watched publication selections, and publication revision-check metadata are stored locally on your device.

DAF e-Publishing Network Access

When you open or refresh the Live e-Pubs screen, open an official publication, or choose to save a publication, the app connects directly to the Department of the Air Force e-Publishing websites over HTTPS. As with a normal website request, those government-operated services may receive technical information such as your IP address, request time, and standard network headers. The app developer does not operate those services and does not receive or retain their server logs.

Offline Publication Copies

If you choose “Save to Files for Offline Use,” the app downloads the selected official PDF and presents Apple’s file exporter. You choose where the document is stored and can delete it later using the Files app. The app does not upload the saved document or your selected storage location.

Analytics & Advertising

The app does not include advertising SDKs and does not enable third-party analytics by default.

Data Sharing

The app developer does not sell your data or share locally stored training progress with third parties. Direct requests to linked government websites are described above.

User-Initiated Sharing

If you choose to use the in-app “Bug Report” or “Feature Request” sharing tools, the information you enter is shared only when you explicitly choose a destination (for example, Mail, Messages, or another app).

Data Retention

On-device training progress remains on your device until you reset progress in the app or uninstall the app.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
