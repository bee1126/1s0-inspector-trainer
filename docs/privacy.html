Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Safety Inspector Trainer (“the app”) handles information when you use it.

Information We Collect

The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information by default. Training progress (such as completed modules and scores) is stored locally on your device.

Analytics & Advertising

The app does not include advertising SDKs and does not enable third-party analytics by default.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell or share your data with third parties.

User-Initiated Sharing

If you choose to use the in-app “Bug Report” or “Feature Request” sharing tools, the information you enter is shared only when you explicitly choose a destination (for example, Mail, Messages, or another app).

Data Retention

On-device training progress remains on your device until you reset progress in the app or uninstall the app.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
